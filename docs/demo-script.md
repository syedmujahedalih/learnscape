# Three-minute demo

1. Open the titration sample and show a static source becoming a blueprint.
2. Generate the titration world; add acid near equivalence and switch among lab, molecular, curve, and equation views.
3. Open Ohm’s Law; double voltage, then double resistance.
4. Open statistics; move the outlier and contrast mean and median.
5. Paste a new source into Local Llama mode and show the proposed blueprint or honest unsupported state.
